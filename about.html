RUTGERS STEAM

We are Rutgers STEAM (STEM + ARTS), part of a national, student-led movement to incorporate and STEM fields with arts and humanities subjects and explore all the possibilities there are. Our organization seeks to explore the many ways in which STEM subjects intersect with humanities subjects; through interactive workshops, lecture series, and field trips, we serve to educate and inspire the general public/student body regarding these connections.

We are one of the several national chapters. Visit steamwith.us for more information. :)
